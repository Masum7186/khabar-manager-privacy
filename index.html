We may collect the following types of information:

The information we collect is used to:

Location access is completely optional. It is used only for automatically filling the address field. The app will continue to function even if you deny this permission.

We store your data securely using trusted services such as Firebase. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

We do not sell, trade, or rent users’ personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only when required by law or to provide core app functionality.

Users can update or delete their personal information at any time from within the app. You may also uninstall the app to stop all data collection.

Khabar Manager is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
